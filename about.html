    What is Pomodoro?

    The Pomodoro technique is a time management technique that helps you focus in small bursts, called pomodoros. Breaking your productivity into smaller, more managable sessions helps prevent burnout and reduce procrastination.

    1. Focus on a task for 25 minutes with no distractions
    2. Take a break for 5 minutes
    3. After 4 focus sessions (pomodoros), take a 30 minute break
    4. Repeat as many times as needed

    The Pomodoro technique was invented by Francesco Cirillo in the 1980s when he was struggling to focus in college. He decided to use a tomato kitchen timer to work in 25 minute increments. Pomodoro means "tomato" in Italian.
